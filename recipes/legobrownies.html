Lego Brownies

Description

Don't these Lego Brownies look delicious? And the best part is: they are incredibly simple to make! First, you either make or buy a batch of brownies. Then you make the icing out of powdered sugar and water, spread it out over the brownies, and finish it off with m&ms. Voil√†: the perfect dessert for a themed children's party!

Ingredients

  • Brownies, homemade or store-bought
  • 200g of powdered sugar
  • Food coloring in various m&m's colors
  • m&m's

Steps

  1. Buy or make your favorite brownies. If homemade, let them cool before moving on to the next step!
  2. Make the icing by dumping the powdered sugar into a bowl, and adding little bits of water while stirring until you get a thick, easily spreadable paste
  3. Divide the icing into 3 or 4 batches and put them in separate, smaller bolws
  4. Add a few drops of food coloring to each batch of icing. Choose a different color for each one. Make sure the colors match the m&m's you have!
  5. Spread the icing evenly over the top of your brownies
  6. Add m&m's, logo down, as shown in the picture
